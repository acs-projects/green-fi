Project: Green-Fi

An energy independent, zero carbon footprint WiFi solution

Launched October 31, 2018

In order to achieve energy independence (from gas powered generators) and mobile freedom (freeing ourselves from the grid), ACS has set sights on harnessing the power of the sun with solar energy.

We’d like to introduce to you our lightweight, stripped-down, cost effective, power-efficient prototype proposal.  The specs are drawn, the math calculated, the hardware carefully chosen.

OUTSIDE

THE GLORY

What’s on the pole?

An access point, a solar panel, an enclosure, and the glorious ACS flag.

INSIDE

THE GUTS

What’s inside the enclosure?

Two batteries in parallel, a solar controller, a power inverter, a cable modem, and a router.

  • Diesel Fuel costs $3.39 at the 7-11 gas station across the street from 700 N Rancho Dr.
  • The Herc Rental diesel generator, DCA45SSIU4F, costs $250/day or $$650/wk or $1550/mo to rent.  This cost is not included in the ticker.

  • The total fuel capacity of the DCA45SSIU4F is 79 gallons.  The fuel consumption rate is 2.93 GPH.  It costs $267.81 for a full tank that will run for 27 hours.
  • Each gallon of diesel fuel produces, on average, 22.4 lb of C02.
